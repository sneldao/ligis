AI agents are getting smarter — but they're still anonymous.
A wallet isn't an identity. A prompt isn't a permission.
Ligis fixes that. Portable on-chain identity, plus signed, revocable capability credentials.
The same capability hash is recognized on Casper and on EVM.
On Casper Testnet, an agent mints its own identity.
The Trust Steward signs an EIP-712 credential, and the Casper contract recovers the issuer on-chain using secp256k1.
The Steward runs the full autonomous loop: boot, reason, gate, act, record — all on Casper.
That credential unlocks a paid x402 endpoint — credential verified, payment settled on Casper.
Ligis is live on Casper Testnet. Portable trust for the agent economy.
